Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?
Typically, a standard business website takes 1-2 weeks to complete. Complex websites with custom features may take 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EcoCash, Innbucks, and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided after order confirmation.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we have a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our work, we'll either revise it or provide a full refund.
Can I update my website after it's built?
Absolutely! We provide training and documentation. We also offer maintenance packages if you prefer us to handle updates.
Do you provide support after project completion?
Yes! We provide 30 days of free support after project completion. Extended support packages are also available.
